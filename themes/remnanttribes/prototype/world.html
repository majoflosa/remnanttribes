These lands have been inhabited since before there is any record of any kind. Ancient lore from different parts tell several stories as to how this world and everything in it came to be. Populations of diverse races too numerous to track have come and gone, through times of peace and times of turmoil. In recent years, however, many are crumbling down at the feet of an immense power wielded by the Venthaal Empire. Fewer and fewer have managed to hold on to existence, finding refuge in lands unknown.

Agenes

Fleeing the incessant threat of the Venthaal Empire's persecution, a small group of settlers led by Kyoji have made these remote, mountainous grounds their new home. A wide river and nearby woods provide more than enough resources for food and shelter. Home of Kyoji, Beor, and Iva, this is the settlement the Empire's scouts search for most desperately of all the settlements that remain free from the Empire's domain.

The Meadow

Home of the Feles tribe. This vast open field is ideal for the Feles' outstanding hunting abilities. Their settlement is a massive cluster of small huts, which remain mostly vacant due to the race's preference for the open field. They are natural-born hunters, and though practiced mostly to feed their population, their skills have allowed them to easily prey on unfortunate scouts who lose their way too far into their tall pastures. Their chieftain guards these lands fiercely, and constantly reminds of the dangers outside of the Meadow to discourage anyone from leaving it.

Imperial District

The capital of the Venthaal Empire. This formidable city is where the plunderers, bandits, and all sorts of scoundrel first came together centuries ago to become an organized and feared troop that conquered lands for ages to come. With riches taken from neighboring villages and cities, they placed the first stones of what was to become the headquarters of a powerful reign that engulfs much of the present day Known World. Every decision to invade and engage in battle against surrounding settlements is taken from the Emperor's Citadel that towers above all other structures in the heart of the island. Watch towers all over the district make an attack on this site unthinkable. Around the Emperor's Citadel, a large population sustains itself selling goods, crafting rare jewels, and forging weapons - all benefiting from the lavish whims of the local rich families.
