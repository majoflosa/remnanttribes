Director’s Commentary: Prologue

April 15, 2018April 15, 2018 by Mauricio Flores

Pages 0 – 6

Is there anything harder than writing an opening scene?  The story I have planned for this comic is quite long, with a lot of history of wars and legends in the background, and a vast and diverse fantasy world. One thing I’ve given special attention to is how, when, and where to start. Even though this story has gone through a lot of changes since I first conceived it in 2012, I’ve stuck with a beginning very similar to what I came up with originally – Kyoji sitting alone in a cabin lit by fire, while captions set up the situation he and his people are in, from his point of view.
